Sindh Rural Support Organization

Established in 2003, the Sindh Rural Support Organization is a not-for-profit organization registered under Section 42 of the Companies Act 2017. The SRSO is present in 15 districts. The mandate of SRSO is to alleviate poverty by harnessing the people’s potential and to undertake development activities in Sindh....

Sindh Union Council and Community Economic Strengthening Support (SUCCESS) Programme


SUCCESS is a six-year long (2015-2021) programme funded by the European Union (EU) and SRSO is implementing this Program in two districts of Sindh, namely: Kambar Shahdadkot and Larkana. The specific objective is to stimulate local CDD initiatives to reduce poverty in eight poor rural districts of Sindh.....
